Company

Koupon Media provides a Digital Campaign Management (DCM) platform for retailers and brands to unify the creation, distribution, redemption, and analysis of digital coupons and promotions. Koupon Media's elegant business-to-business platform is a single-source solution for promotional marketers that includes; campaign management, multi channel distribution, coupon clearing, and consumer analytics.

TJ Person, CEO

TJ is a seasoned entrepreneur with 15+ years of mobile experience, notably as a co-founder and president of Mango Mobile. In 2005 Mango Mobile raised $1.5 million and after 2 years as the fastest growing mobile marketing company Omnicom Group acquired it in February 2007. TJ spent several years with Omnicon where Mango continues as a leader in the mobile space. TJ is a co-founder of Koupon Media.

Scott Hutchinson, CTO

Scott has 12 + years of experience in wireless mobile application services and ecommerce with a strong background in enterprise level application development and implementation. Scott has served as lead developer on web and mobile applications for major global brands including Pepsi, Levi's, Slurpee, Cars.com, MTV, VH-1 and "Gossip Girl". He was the first developer hired at Mango Mobile and subsequently helped to build the entire platform. Scott is a co-founder of Koupon Media.
